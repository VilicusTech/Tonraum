Description

The TRC-1125 is the reason we are here today, at Tonraum. This Microphone is the project that started it all. With a pristene Steel finish, coated in a deep black, this beautiful feat of engineering represents us, as a company. With Audio Quality on par with - if not better than - competing brands, this Microphones goal is to be able to catch everything. From soft, whispered vocals, to loud and energetic screams, this Microphone boasts the capability to handle both. Using a conventional balanced 3-Way XLR connector means no signal, or noise, gets lost on the way to your interface, allowing for clean, uninterrupted recording. The built in mesh stops plosives from destroying the capsule, and halts any interferance too.

This is the 1125. This is Tonraum.
