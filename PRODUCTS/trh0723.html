Description

The TRH-0723 Studio Monitor Headphones, are the highest quality Headphone we can supply. With an almost completely flat and accurate Frequency Response, it delivers the best, most true-to-life sound we can possibly offer. Using a Semi Open-Back design allows for great In-Head audio quality, while also allowing Monitoring from a partial outside/stadium point of view. Covered in premium fur and leather, it is the most comfortable headset you will ever wear. The fur pads are almost 3/4 of an inch thick, allowing for hours of studio use without discomfort. With a wishbone suspension design, they can flex to any headshape, allowing for great compatibility with any extra users. The cord is thick and woven for a premium feel, also granting it the toughness required for a Studio Headset.
